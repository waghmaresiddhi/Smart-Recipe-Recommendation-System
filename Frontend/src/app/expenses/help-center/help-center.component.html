Manage your expenses effectively with these steps:

  • 1. Add an Expense: Click the "Add Expense" button on your dashboard and fill in the required fields such as category, amount, and date. Then, submit the form to save the expense.
  • 2. Edit an Expense: Navigate to the "Expense History" section, where you can view all your past expenses. Click the edit icon next to any entry to modify the details.
  • 3. Delete an Expense: If you want to remove an expense, click the delete icon next to the entry in the "Expense History" section.

Generate reports and analyze your spending patterns:

  • 1. View Reports: In the "Reports" section, you can generate summaries of your expenses by month, category, or custom date ranges.
  • 2. Export Data: Use the "Export" feature to download reports in CSV or PDF format for external analysis.
  • 3. Visualize Data: The dashboard includes graphical representations (bar charts, pie charts) of your expenses, powered by Chart.js. These graphs help you quickly understand your spending patterns over time.

For example, a bar graph will show monthly expenses categorized into food, transport, utilities, etc., helping you identify areas of excessive spending.
